Kindergarten Kastanien

This prototype, created in Marvel App, was created as part of my internship on the 8th semester.

This prototype displays my design idea for a redesign of the kindergarten Kastanien website. The design is aimed at displaying the core values of the kindergarten in order to stand out from the competition in the town, Vester Hassing, which has a total of four kindergartens. The transparency of the website is enhanced by displaying the kindergarten staff with a picture, contact information, and a personal description.
